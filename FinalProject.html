Final Project

Welcome to Chris and Anthony's Final Project

This final project is a khan acadmey version of an online air hockey game. The objective is to beat the computerized opponent to
five points. You control your paddle by using the left and right arrow keys to move it back and forth. There is an instructions
page on the game aswell that tells the player how to play the game. When the ball hits the walls or the baddle it will bounce off
going in the opposite direction. There are sounds for when a goal is scored, and there is an end screen once five points are reach
telling you who won.
